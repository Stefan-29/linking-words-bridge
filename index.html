Fun with Linking Words & Conjunctions

Welcome, Word Explorers!

Today we're going to learn about linking words and conjunctions!

These special words help us connect our ideas when we write and speak!

What Are Linking Words?

Linking words are like bridges that connect our ideas!

I like ice cream and chocolate.

I want to play outside but it's raining.

I did my homework so I can watch TV.

Types of Linking Words

Adding Ideas

and, also, too

Contrasting

but, however

Showing Reason

because, so

Diary Examples

Let's look at different writing styles in diaries!

Informal Diary

Dear Diary,

Today was awesome! I played soccer and I scored a goal! My team was happy because we won the game. I wanted to get ice cream but it started raining, so we went home instead.

Formal Writing

Weekly Report:

The class completed the math test and started a new science project. However, three students were absent. Therefore, they will need to make up the test next week.

Can you see the differences between informal and formal writing?

Mini Debate

"Should school uniforms be mandatory?"

YES

I think uniforms are good because they make everyone equal.

Also, uniforms help us focus on learning.

Furthermore, they save time in the morning!

NO

I don't like uniforms because they stop us from being unique.

However, I understand why some people like them.

In conclusion, I think we should choose our clothes.
